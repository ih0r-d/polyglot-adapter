# Code of Conduct

This project follows the principles of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).  
Our goal is to maintain a professional, inclusive, and productive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Being constructive in feedback and reviews
- Gracefully accepting criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

### Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, personal attacks, or insults
- Discriminatory remarks or hate speech
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Other conduct which could be reasonably considered inappropriate

---

## Enforcement
Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.  
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an Issue with the details.
(For sensitive reports, contact the maintainers via a private channel listed in the repository.)
